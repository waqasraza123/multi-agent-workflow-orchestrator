# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, constructive, and professional collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- communicate clearly and respectfully
- focus feedback on the work
- assume good intent while staying direct
- welcome technically grounded disagreement
- keep discussions useful for contributors and maintainers

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, threats, or intimidation
- discriminatory or abusive language
- doxxing or invasion of privacy
- bad faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or reviews
- personal attacks instead of technical discussion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that do not meet this standard.
